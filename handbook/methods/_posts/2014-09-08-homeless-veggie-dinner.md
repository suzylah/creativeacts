<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><strong><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">Planning the logistics</span></strong></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">Venue: find a place that can host a dinner, preferably for a good cause. Make sure to find out, what are the rules of the place (how many people, what's the cooking equipment, is there enough cutlery, are there lavatories, will there be any stuff helping etc). Its good to sign an agreement about cooperation to avoid further misunderstandings.</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">Food: map the places/cafes/bakeries in the neighborhood that might have leftovers. Establish connection, invite for cooperation.</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">Developing task list: depending from the scale of the event, its still good to have an overview about the tasks that need to be done, so, when it comes to the volunteers, they have a concrete idea of what needs to be done.</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><strong><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">Gather a crew</span></strong></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">This is the step to gather the team of volunteers, that will actually carry out the practical tasks, spreading the information, serving the people, doing the tasks in the kitchen, helping with cleaning.</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">Put out the call in the channels the people you want to involve (to volunteer), hang out. In case of the Homeless Veggie Dinner, the main channels are mouth-to-mouth (volunteers bring new volunteers), Facebook, Couchsurfing and other on- and off-line communities, where the "international crowd" is used to hang out.</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">Ask for responses to sign up for concrete tasks, tell exact times, when the people need to be there. Communicate through the channels used, if there are tasks still open or when its all booked.</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><strong><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">Promotion</span></strong></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">So the purpose of the original event is to connect homeless and poor people with the people from other social groups. That means that 2 completely different channels of information shall be used (for ex, to reach the homeless people, using internet is not necessarily appropriate): put flyers in other soup kitchens in the neighborhood or in the places where the people are hanging out. Actually the personal invitation works the best and a flyer is just a reminder of time&amp;space. Consider the same channels as for searching the volunteers to be used.</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><strong><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">Organizing the dinner</span></strong></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">Still a deal of logistics needs to be done: </span></p>
<ul>
	<li style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px none; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;,Helvetica,Arial,sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.6364px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">a plan of where, on what times and how the food will be picked up. Where it will be stored, if needed;</span></li>
	<li style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px none; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;,Helvetica,Arial,sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.6364px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">a plan of cooking - what needs to be chopped when, etc.</span>;</li>
	<li style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px none; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;,Helvetica,Arial,sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.6364px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">in the dinner its nice to put out some donation boxes for the food, as well as in the original event there are always "Menu" printed with the choices - all the guests, regardless homeless or not, are served by the volunteers. As well, signs outside to make it easier to find the place. Equip the place with signs of rules if there are any special.</span></li>
</ul>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><strong><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">Event</span></strong></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">Its all not so difficult, gets better every time but requires some sort of management and facilitation. Its nice to have a person who is in charge of logistics etc, a person, who is coordinating the volunteers and and another team member, who has an overview about the actual cooking process.</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">Make sure, the required anonymity is there - the target group might be sensitive, if there are cameras around or something.</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">As well, a nice dinner can always be improved by a free shop, free concert by a friend of yours, etc.</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><strong><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">Reflection</span></strong><br>
	<span style="font-family: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">If possible/necessary, the sharing of experiences between the volunteers after the event definitely contributes to the team dynamics, motivates them to come back as well as ensures improvement of the process.</span></p>
