<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">The “Home- made” festival started in Asturias in 2010 as a one night festival. The festival is a participatory, self-managed artistic and cultural event where some inhabitants of the the city open their houses/flats to the public and for one night transform them into alternative cultural spaces with a diverse range of activities: performances, workshops, concerts, theatre, etc...</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">Through their activities the festival want to connect the local community, to create new alternative cultural channels in the city and to bring art and creativity to public spaces and to other type of public.</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">Participants of the festival will wear an apron as a identification object.</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">To facilitate the participation and to prevent overfilled activities, the program is divided in 2 or 3 routes. Each route is composed of 3 or 4 locations with 1 hour time slots. At the end all routes meet together in one square of the city where a final party with concerts is held.</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">Each route has as well their own name and their own animator who is in charge to lead and motivate the group and be the timekeeper so people don´t get lost for hours in one place and never appear in the next one!</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">This festival won´t be possible without the active participation of people from the local community who propose activities, build the festival setting and promote it.</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">This festival is free, open and 100% replicable! Feel free to do so and to add whatever you feel could suit your local community.&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">A video from last edition can be found here:&nbsp;<a href="https://vimeo.com/102205752" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(0, 113, 179); text-decoration: none;">https://vimeo.com/102205752</a></span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><strong><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">Kick- off meeting</span></strong></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">Pass on information through all the channels and host a kick off meeting with the people that are motivated to be involved. Spread the call for the meeting as far as possible - unexpected participants can contribute even more. We recommend to organize the meeting in a cozy place to create a nice atmosphere to present and discuss about the activities.</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">During the meeting make a <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">comprehensive</span> intro to the festival for participants to get an idea what is it all about. You can use photos or videos from past editions.</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;"> Take time for presentations - making new people feel comfortable with the group is highly important. Collect the interests of the people: will people be interested in opening their houses, interested to realize activities or willing to help in the overall organization, which concerns they have. After make a first time plan and divide tasks in the group.</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">At the end of this meeting it is important to have an overview of places and activities. It normally happens that you have more places than activities or the opposite so it´s great to have an overview to know what you should look for.</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;"><strong><span style="font-family: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit;">Preparing the routes</span></strong></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">This involves deciding where to host each activity, taking in account the type of activity and the size of the place and connecting the host with the workshop giver.</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">Decide when each activity will take place depending on the host needs or where the place is situated in the city. Tip: we usually choose the most far-away spaces at the beginning when people are fresh and happy to walk. An animator of the route is a great help! Usually choose the name for the animator which is related with a homely object that we can use during the festival to get the group together.</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">Design maps with the routes and place, take care people can find it.</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><strong>The festival</strong></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">2 hours before the festival starts: last check of needs with the hosts and the workshop givers, building the meeting point using the homely decorations, getting yourself ready and cozy with your favorite pajamas and hair-curlers, finishing the aprons for the participants and bring the route maps to the meeting point.</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 10px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; line-height: 20px; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline; color: rgb(59, 59, 59);"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-size: 13.63636302947998px; vertical-align: baseline;">In the beginning of the festival, make it clear to the participants which routes there are and who the animators of each route are. Divide people in routes checking that the sizes of the groups are more or less equal and enjoy the activities!</span></p>
